Miami Crime Simulator 3D

Miami Paradise, Let's Go Catch Some Criminals

Miami Crime Simulator 3D is an action-packed open-world game that plunges players into the heart of a bustling city teeming with crime and chaos. Set in the vibrant streets of Miami, players take on the role of a criminal protagonist as they embark on a variety of missions and activities, including car theft, bank robberies, and street brawls. With its realistic graphics, immersive gameplay, and vast open-world environment, Miami Crime Simulator 3D offers players the ultimate sandbox experience where they can unleash their inner criminal mastermind.

Choose Your Character

Start by selecting your character from a roster of criminal protagonists, each with their own unique abilities and traits. Choose the character that best suits your play style and preferences.

Explore the City

Once you've selected your character, explore the sprawling city of Miami at your leisure. Navigate through the streets, alleys, and landmarks of the city as you search for opportunities to commit crimes and earn rewards.

Complete Missions

Throughout the game, you'll encounter various missions and objectives to complete. These missions may involve tasks such as stealing cars, robbing banks, eliminating rival gang members, and evading law enforcement. Complete missions to earn money, experience points, and unlock new content.  

Commit Crimes

Engage in a variety of criminal activities such as carjacking, burglary, and street fights to earn money and increase your reputation as a notorious criminal. Use weapons such as guns, knives, and explosives to intimidate enemies and achieve your goals.

Customize Your Character

Visit the in-game store to customize your character with new outfits, weapons, and accessories. Spend your hard-earned money to upgrade your arsenal and improve your chances of success in the criminal underworld.

Evade Law Enforcement

Be mindful of law enforcement officers patrolling the city, as committing crimes will attract their attention and lead to pursuit. Use stealth, evasion tactics, and fast vehicles to evade capture and escape from police chases.

Engage in Street Battles:

Encounter rival gangs and criminal organizations throughout the city and engage in intense street battles to assert your dominance. Use cover, tactics, and firepower to defeat your enemies and expand your territory in the criminal underworld.

Drive Vehicles

Explore the city in a variety of vehicles ranging from sports cars and motorcycles to trucks and helicopters. Use vehicles to quickly traverse the city, escape from police pursuit, and carry out daring heists and getaways.

Earn Rewards

Earn money, experience points, and other rewards by completing missions, committing crimes, and defeating enemies. Use your rewards to purchase new weapons, vehicles, and upgrades to enhance your criminal empire.

Become the Kingpin

Rise through the ranks of the criminal underworld and become the ultimate kingpin of Miami. Dominate the city, expand your territory, and establish your reputation as the most feared and respected criminal in Miami Crime Simulator 3D.
