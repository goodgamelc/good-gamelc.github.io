Mad Truck Challenge Special

Racing action shooting. Let's conquer now

Mad Truck Challenge Special is a high-octane racing game that puts players behind the wheel of powerful monster trucks in a series of adrenaline-fueled races. With its vibrant graphics, explosive gameplay, and customizable trucks, Mad Truck Challenge Special offers an exhilarating gaming experience for players of all ages. Race against rival drivers, perform stunts, and upgrade your truck to become the ultimate champion in this action-packed racing adventure.

Select Your Truck

Start by selecting your truck from a range of available options, each with its own unique characteristics and abilities. Choose the truck that best suits your style of racing and preferences.

Choose Your Race

Once you've selected your truck, choose your race from a variety of tracks and environments, including deserts, mountains, and urban landscapes. Each race offers its own challenges and obstacles to overcome.

Customize Your Truck

Before starting the race, customize your truck by upgrading its performance and appearance. Use the in-game currency earned from races to purchase upgrades such as engine enhancements, nitro boosts, and armor improvements to make your truck faster and more durable.  

Start Your Engines

Once you're ready, start your engines and prepare to race against rival drivers in a series of intense competitions. Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to accelerate, steer, and brake your truck as you navigate through the track.

Collect Power-ups

Along the way, collect power-ups and bonuses scattered throughout the track to gain an advantage over your opponents. Power-ups may include nitro boosts for increased speed, shields for protection against obstacles, and weapons for attacking rival trucks.

Perform Stunts

Show off your skills by performing stunts and tricks during the race. Use ramps and obstacles to launch your truck into the air and perform flips, spins, and other aerial maneuvers to earn extra points and boost your score.

Avoid Obstacles

Watch out for obstacles such as rocks, barriers, and other vehicles as you race through the track. Colliding with obstacles will slow you down and cause damage to your truck, so stay focused and maneuver carefully to avoid them.

Defeat Rival Drivers

Race against rival drivers and do whatever it takes to beat them to the finish line. Use your speed, skill, and firepower to outmaneuver and outgun your opponents, leaving them in the dust as you race towards victory.

Complete Challenges

Throughout the game, you'll encounter various challenges and objectives to complete. These challenges may include winning races, earning high scores, or performing specific stunts. Completing challenges will unlock rewards and help you progress in the game.

Achieve Victory

Race your way to victory and become the ultimate champion in Mad Truck Challenge Special. Dominate the competition, upgrade your truck, and prove yourself as the best monster truck driver in the world in this thrilling racing adventure.
